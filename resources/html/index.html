Thursday A collection calendar

If you don't know which round you are on, you can visit myhome to look up your property to find the right calendar.

Due to operational issues, we have had to cancel food waste collections in The Leighs, Great Waltham, Pleshey, Ford End and North End and green box collections in Boreham on Friday 1 July 2022. We have rescheduled the collections for Saturday 2 July 2022. You can view a list of affected roads.

We are also currently behind with our normal schedule for recycling collections from flats. We apologise for any inconvenience.

You can download the Thursday A collection calendar, or view the upcoming dates below.
